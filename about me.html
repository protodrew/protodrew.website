About Me

I am Drew, a Nonbinary software developer. I have been online since as long as I can remember, and have been working here for around 5 years.

I have written about video games, Podcasted, developed games, contributed to FOSS, and freelanced in web design and video editing.

I am a university student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, where I am double majoring in Interactive Media and Game Development, and Computer Science.

After college, I wish to utilize my skills to help make the internet more efficient, accessible, and functional. I love every aspect of the internet from the massive social network mastodon down to the tiniest library that helps render a certain kind of shape.
