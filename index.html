ProtoDrew

FOSS Enthusiast, Queer Activist, Ecosocialist, Nonbinary person

I have been online since as long as I can remember, and have been working here for around 5 years. I have written about video games, Podcasted, developed games, contributed to FOSS, and freelanced in web design and video editing.

I am a soon to be university student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, where I am double majoring in Interactive Media and Game Development, and Computer Science.

After college, I wish to utilize my skills to help make the internet more efficient, accessible, and functional. I love every aspect of the internet from the massive social network mastodon down to the tiniest library that helps render a certain kind of shape.

Philosophy

I believe in an open internet, open borders, sustainable technology, and an egalitarian society. This greatly informs the projects I take on, and the popular tech mantra of move fast and break things is lost on me.

I geniunely believe in technology's ability to make the world a better place, but it cannot be our only solution. If we must halt progress in order to accomplish this, we must prioritize the well being of every person rather than prioritizing growth and production for its own sake.

My Work

I began my work when I was 13 years old and I began doing freelance video editing for independant game developers. I was deeply invested in the video games industry and found a great community in independant games. The first real work I did for any long amount of time was writing, video producing, and doing web design for a small outlet called CFN Gaming, which is unfortunately now inactive.

I then served as the editor in chief for Geeknosh (now defunct), where I designed the website through wordpress, and began dedicating a lot of my time to learning to code. I worked with gamemaker studio and Unity for a few years, and began taking an interest in open source software. While I haven't made a ton of contributions so far, I am studying rust to be able to help with Lemmy, and other activity pub social media.

In addition to my freelance writing, video editing, and video production, I have also worked in web programming, both via site builders and with html/css alone (like this site). I keep my personal site intentionally simple to allow anybody to access it, but if you want to contact me and see a better example of my skillset email me at proto_drew at protonmail dot com (just replace the at and dot with their respective symbols).



I am learning and growing every day, and I try to constantly look for people who are better than me so I can learn from them. I post all of my projects and updates on them on my mastodon @protodrew@floss.social
